# Market Research Search Terms

Use these to research organic content strategies on TikTok/Instagram/YouTube.

## Direct Competitors
- MenuFit
- Cole Kosco
- macro friendly restaurant
- macro friendly fast food

## Content Format Research
- what I eat in a day macros
- high protein fast food
- low calorie fast food order
- healthy chipotle order
- what to order at [restaurant] for weight loss
- bulking on a budget
- cutting diet eating out
- macro friendly meals

## Hook/Trend Research
- restaurant macro hacks
- fast food but make it healthy
- eating out on a diet
- gym bro eats
- bodybuilder fast food
- protein hack
- calorie comparison restaurant

## Pain Point Content
- eating out while tracking macros
- staying on diet with friends
- restaurant ruined my cut
- hidden calories restaurant

## Viral Format Ideas
- "I ate [restaurant] for a week while cutting"
- "ranking [restaurant] menu by protein"
- "foods I never order vs always order"
- "what I actually get at [restaurant]"

---

## Tips
- Look for videos with high views but low follower accounts - that's the algorithm working, not just existing audience
- Pay attention to hooks in the first 2 seconds
- Note what CTAs they use and engagement patterns
